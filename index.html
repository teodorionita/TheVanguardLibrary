THE VANGUARD LIBRARY

Welcome to Our Digital Library

This is a curated collection of book recommendations from our reading group. We believe in sharing knowledge and discovering new perspectives through literature.

How It Works

  • Browse our collection in the Library section
  • Submit new recommendations with your access token
  • Share reading locations and resources
  • Build our collective knowledge base

Getting Started

To contribute:

  • Get your access token from the group administrator
  • Go to the Submit Entry page
  • Fill in the book details and your thoughts
  • Preview your entry before submission

Guidelines

When submitting entries:

  • Include thoughtful notes about why you recommend the book
  • Add links to legal reading sources when available
  • Mention any relevant themes or topics
  • Note any content warnings if applicable
